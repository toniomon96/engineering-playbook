## Weekly DM target

Per the v2 90-day roadmap: **three referral DMs per week** during the first soft outreach phase (weeks 3–6). The dashboard's stat card surfaces this — `0 / 3 DMs sent` shows in warning amber until the count reaches three.

After the first audit converts, target shifts to one quality DM per week plus follow-ups on existing conversations. The mass outreach phase ends when the funnel starts filling.

## What gets logged here

Everything outbound that's intentionally trying to drive a referral or conversation. Specifically:

- DMs to potential referrers (asking for introductions)
- Direct outreach to potential clients
- Follow-ups on prior conversations
- Cold-warm intros via mutual connections

What does NOT get logged here:

- Inbound conversations (those become pipeline files)
- General networking with no specific ask
- Social media engagement (likes, comments)
- Replies to your own posts

The discipline: if you sent it with intent, it's a row. If it was conversational, it isn't.
