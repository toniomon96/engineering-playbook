## Discipline

The list is curated, not aspirational. If a name has been here for 90 days without an outreach event in `log/outreach.md`, move it to Tier 3 or remove it. The list is for active conversion, not historical relationship inventory.

Quarterly review: read the full list, update statuses, prune stale names, add new tier 1 candidates from the last quarter's networking.

The Tier 1 list is the prompt. The console's "DMs sent this week" stat sitting at `0 / 3` means: open this file, pick three names, send three DMs.
